# Documentation Formatting Rules

## 1. Third-Party Citations and Attributions
* Keep all primary titles, document headers, and high-level architecture section titles generic and focused on the actual technical implementation names (e.g., "Safety Guidance" or "Geodesic Path Guidance").
* Relegate any specific citations to third-party inspirations, repositories, or external creators (such as "AlphaPhoenix" or specific YouTube URLs) to the **References**, **Acknowledgements**, or **Attributions** section at the bottom of the file. Do not feature these in page titles or major headings.
